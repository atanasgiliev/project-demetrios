Our Mission

Where classical scholarship
meets the digital age

Project Demetrios develops precision tools for the close reading of Ancient Greek poetry. We believe that computational analysis should serve the humanist — illuminating patterns invisible to the naked eye while remaining fully transparent in its methods.

Every tool runs entirely in the browser via Pyodide, requiring no installation, no server, and no dependency on external APIs. The result is portable, reproducible scholarship.

Each tool in the Project Demetrios suite targets a distinct dimension of Greek poetic technique — from the phonological texture of vowel sequences to the large-scale architecture of rhetorical repetition.
